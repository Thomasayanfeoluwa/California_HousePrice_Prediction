About Us

Our California House Price Predictor uses advanced machine learning to estimate property values based on key factors like median income, house age, and location. Built on the California Housing dataset, our model provides accurate predictions to help homeowners, buyers, and investors make informed decisions.

We are committed to simplifying real estate analysis with a user-friendly tool that delivers reliable results.

How It Works

Our prediction tool analyzes eight key features to estimate house prices:

  • Median Income: The median income of the block group (in $10,000s).
  • House Age: The median age of houses in the block.
  • Average Rooms: The average number of rooms per dwelling.
  • Average Bedrooms: The average number of bedrooms per dwelling.
  • Population: The total population in the block group.
  • Average Occupancy: The average number of occupants per household.
  • Latitude & Longitude: The geographic coordinates of the block.

Enter these details in the form above, and our model will predict the median house value for the specified location.
